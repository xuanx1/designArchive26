A quantitative visualisation of fish biodiversity drawn from the Smithsonian Institution's natural history collections. The work explores taxonomic distribution, habitat ranges, and collection density � rendering the ocean's extraordinary diversity as a navigable sea of interlocking data, where every point represents a specimen catalogued for science.
